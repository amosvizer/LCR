## 5.10 ABOUT / COMPANY (/about)

**Page H1:** About LCR Aero Group

### Our Story

“Since 2013, the LCR Aero Group has provided unparalleled certification, regulatory, and safety compliance consulting to aviation organizations worldwide. Founded by Lawrence “Larry” Richards, a career aviation professional with 50 years of experience including 26 years with the FAA, LCR was built on a simple premise: the best certification consultants are the ones who have sat in the FAA’s chair.

Our team is composed entirely of former FAA Aviation Safety Inspectors and seasoned industry professionals. We don’t just know the regulations — we know how the FAA interprets them, how inspectors evaluate compliance, and what it takes to earn approval with confidence.

In 2024, we became one of the first boutique certification consultancies to integrate Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning into our regulatory compliance workflow. Our AI-enhanced tools don’t replace human expertise — they amplify it, ensuring every citation is verified, every cross-reference is accurate, and every submission meets the highest standard of quality.

Today, we support flight operations, dispatch, maintenance, HAZMAT, inflight, ground operations, and passenger and cargo handling for air carriers, repair stations, and agricultural operators worldwide.”

### Our Mission

“To provide aviation operators with the most accurate, efficient, and reliable certification and compliance consulting available — combining the insight of former FAA inspectors with the precision of AI-enhanced tools to deliver programs that the FAA accepts with confidence.”

### Our Values

- **Precision:** Every document, every citation, every procedure is verified and accurate.
- **Transparency:** We keep you informed at every stage. No surprises. No hidden costs.
- **Efficiency:** Your timeline is our timeline. We work to get you certified as fast as responsibly possible.
- **Safety:** Safety is not a line item. It is embedded in everything we do, from SMS design to manual development.
- **Innovation:** We invest in AI and technology because precision at scale is the future of regulatory compliance.

### Template for Case Studies (Develop from Client History)

**Example 1 — Initial Part 121 Certification:** Challenge: A startup cargo airline needed to obtain their Part 121 certificate on an aggressive timeline to meet contractual obligations. Approach: LCR deployed a team of 4 SMEs (flight operations, maintenance, dispatch, HAZMAT) and used AI-enhanced documentation tools to develop the complete manual system and DCT submissions. Client commentary (if available).

**Example 2 — New Aircraft Type Addition:** Challenge: An established carrier needed to add a new fleet type to their OpSpecs within 90 days to meet seasonal demand. Approach: LCR managed the MCPD process, updated all affected manuals, developed crew training curricula, and coordinated with the FSDO. Result: New type approved 2 weeks ahead of schedule.

**Example 3 — Part 145 Repair Station:** Challenge: An MRO organization needed FAA Part 145 certification to expand their service offerings. Approach: LCR developed the complete RSM, QCM, and capability list, and managed the FAA evaluation process. Result: Certification achieved with zero findings.
